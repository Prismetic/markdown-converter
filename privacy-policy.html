Privacy Policy

Markdown Converter Chrome Extension — Last updated: June 27, 2026

Overview

Markdown Converter is a browser extension that converts files (DOCX, PDF, XLSX, HTML, CSV, JSON, XML, TXT, MD, PPTX) to Markdown format entirely within your browser. No file content, metadata, or usage information ever leaves your device.

Data Collection

We collect no data of any kind. Specifically:

  • Files you open are processed in-browser using WebAssembly and JavaScript. They are never uploaded or transmitted to any server.
  • We do not collect analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or usage statistics.
  • We do not use cookies, localStorage, IndexedDB, or any other persistent storage.
  • We do not make any outbound network requests.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions required to show a popup interface. It holds no host permissions and cannot access any website you visit. All processing occurs within the extension popup's sandboxed context.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with, communicate with, or transmit data to any third-party service, API, or analytics platform.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.
